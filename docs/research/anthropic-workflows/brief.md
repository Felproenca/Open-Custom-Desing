# Research Brief: Anthropic Workflows

**Question:** What are Anthropic's workflow patterns and agentic architectures? How do they work, what are the design principles, practical applications, and how do they compare to alternatives?

**Scope:**
- Anthropic's official guidance on building effective agents and workflows
- The workflow taxonomy (prompt chaining, routing, parallelization, orchestrator-workers, evaluator-optimizer)
- Tool use and function calling patterns
- Multi-agent orchestration with Claude
- Real-world applications and case studies
- Comparison with alternatives (OpenAI, LangChain, CrewAI, etc.)
- Latest developments (2025-2026)

**Depth:** standard (3-5 sub-agents, 15+ sources)

**Date:** 2026-07-10

**Audience:** Developer interested in implementing agentic AI workflows

## Angles

1. **Core concepts & taxonomy** — Anthropic's official "Building Effective Agents" guide, workflow patterns (chaining, routing, parallelization, orchestrator-workers, evaluator-optimizer)
2. **Tool use & function calling** — How Claude handles tools, MCP protocol, structured outputs
3. **Multi-agent orchestration** — Patterns for coordinating multiple Claude instances, delegation, state management
4. **Practical applications & case studies** — Real-world implementations, production deployments, success stories
5. **Ecosystem & alternatives** — How Anthropic's approach compares to OpenAI, LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, etc.
