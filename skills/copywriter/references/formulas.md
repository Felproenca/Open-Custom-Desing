# Copywriting Formulas — Quick Reference

## Headline Formulas (from CoppieGPT — 232 frameworks distilled)

### Curiosity Gap
- "The [thing you do] that [surprising result]"
- "Why [common approach] is wrong"
- "What nobody tells you about [topic]"

### Specificity
- "[Number] ways to [achieve result]"
- "How [specific person] [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] [things] that [unexpected benefit]"

### Contrast
- "[Old way] vs [new way]"
- "Not [expected]. [Actual]."
- "Less [negative]. More [positive]."

### Command
- "[Verb] your [noun]"
- "Stop [bad thing]. Start [good thing]."
- "Get [result] without [pain]"

### Question
- "What if [desired outcome] was [easy/possible/free]?"
- "Ready to [achieve result]?"
- "Still [struggling with problem]?"

## Landing Page Copy Structure

### Above the Fold (5-second test)
```
1. What is this?          → Eyebrow + Headline
2. Who is it for?         → Subhead mentions audience
3. Why should I care?     → Benefit in subhead
4. What do I do next?     → CTA button
```

### Feature → Benefit Translation

| Feature (what it is) | Benefit (why they care) |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| "240Hz OLED" | "Every frame renders clean — no ghosting" |
| "4.2mm thin" | "Lighter than most 27-inch monitors" |
| "8 years experience" | "2400+ peças — you're in safe hands" |
| "Sob medida" | "Sua peça, só sua — sem templates" |

### CTA Formulas

**Strong CTAs:**
- [Verb] + [object] + [benefit]: "Agendar sessão gratuita"
- [Verb] + [urgency]: "Começar agora"
- [Verb] + [low commitment]: "Ver exemplos"

**Weak CTAs (avoid):**
- "Submit"
- "Click here"
- "Learn more"
- "Contact us"

### Social Proof Formulas

**Testimonial structure:**
- "Before I [had problem]. After [product], [specific result]."
- "[Name], [role] at [company]"

**Metrics:**
- "[Number]x [improvement]" — "3x mais rápido"
- "[Percentage] of [group]" — "98% dos clientes voltam"
- "[Timeframe] to [result]" — "Resultado em 24 horas"

## Platform-Specific Copy

### Website Hero
- Headline: ≤8 words, bold claim
- Subhead: 15-20 words, expands the promise
- CTA: 2-4 words, action verb

### SaaS Landing
- Headline: What it does (not what it is)
- Subhead: For whom + key benefit
- Features: 3-6 tiles, title + 1-sentence body
- Social proof: Logo bar or 3 testimonials
- CTA: Primary + secondary (lower commitment)

### Portfolio/Agency
- Headline: Philosophy or positioning statement
- Subhead: What you do + for whom
- Work: Images speak, minimal text
- About: Story, not resume
- CTA: "Let's talk" > "Contact"

### E-commerce
- Headline: Product name + category
- Subhead: Key differentiator
- Features: Material, size, care, shipping
- Urgency: "Limited edition" or "Only X left"
- CTA: "Add to cart" or "Buy now"

## Anti-AI-Slop Checklist

- [ ] No "leverage" or "synergy"
- [ ] No "innovative" or "cutting-edge"
- [ ] No "seamless" or "effortless"
- [ ] No "empower" or "transform"
- [ ] No "delve" or "landscape"
- [ ] No "robust" or "comprehensive"
- [ ] No sentences starting with "In today's"
- [ ] No "it's worth noting that"
- [ ] Every sentence earns its place
